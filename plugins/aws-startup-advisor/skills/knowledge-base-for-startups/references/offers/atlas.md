## Incorporate your startup

### Estimated offer value: $150.00

AWS Activate members are eligible to form a company through Stripe Atlas for $350, 30% off the standard price ($150 value).

Incorporate your company and get ready to accept payments, bank, fundraise, and hire. Atlas includes all filing fees and your 1st year of registered agent fees, automatic 83(b) tax election filing, and dozens of perks towards 3rd party products popular with startup founders.
