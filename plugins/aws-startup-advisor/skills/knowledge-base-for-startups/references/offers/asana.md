**Why work with Asana**

Asana is the leading collaborative work management platform in today’s world that enables effective coordination for humans and AI at scale. In today’s distributed work environment teams are working in so many disconnected tools, and despite having so many tools, we still can’t answer the basic question of who is doing what, by when, how and why. Asana makes it easy to orchestrate tasks across teams and connect company-wide goals to the work needed to achieve them. Leverage Asana to initially help you manage day to day projects and tasks, and expand on your usage as you grow your company and team. Asana has flexibility to add licenses as you grow, and you can easily connect to other systems that are imperative to your startup's growth and security (like Google, Okta, HubSpot, or Slack). As a Founder you can limit access to contractors & other employees, and at the same time create the cross-functional collaboration & clarity that is necessary for work. Particularly in today’s remote or hybrid working environments, visibility is necessary to understand productivity and where your team may be stuck to help them tackle roadblocks. Asana standardizes & creates a view of everything going on, so you can prioritize work and make smart business decisions. Allow Asana to be the partner that helps you scale as your company does too.
