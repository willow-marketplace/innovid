## Teaching a generation to read: generative AI's impact on education

**Category:** Trends and Insights

## Overview

Generative AI, trained on user-generated data, is now being used by companies like Ello to revolutionize education. Building a one-to-one teacher was one of the original dreams of AI. It's finally possible to build human-level learning experiences at scale. Learn how one founder, Catalin Voss, CTO of Ello, is using genAI to transform education and the way we interact with learning content with speech perception, language models, and image generation models. Catalin will also share best practices and considerations for startups in any industry on how best to leverage genAI and the enormous impact they can make on their customers' lives.
