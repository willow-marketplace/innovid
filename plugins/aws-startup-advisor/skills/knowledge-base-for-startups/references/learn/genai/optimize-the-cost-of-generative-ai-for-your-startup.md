## Optimize the cost of generative AI for your startup

## Make smart choices now to optimize your generative AI costs later

Implementing a general artificial intelligence (gen AI) solution for your startup can be a significant investment. The choices you make at the outset considerably influence the overall costs of your product or solution over time. This graphic is tailored to help startup founders navigate key factors that contribute to gen AI project costs and provides insights on how to strategically optimize your business to maintain control over your costs.

![Optimize the cost of generative AI for your startup](https://d22k7geae6sy8h.cloudfront.net/files/655b673f127b4f00085e9e1d/8lp6z9b9v-domorewithai.png)

You'll learn about:

1. **Upfront decision impact:** Dive into the pivotal role that early decisions play in shaping the overall costs for gen AI projects.
2. **Cost optimization strategies:** Learn actionable strategies to optimize your gen AI costs without compromising on the quality and effectiveness of your solution.
3. **Influential factors:** See how to identify key factors that influence your budget and how to address them.

Whether you're a seasoned AI practitioner or a newcomer to the generative AI landscape, this guide equips you with the knowledge needed to make informed decisions that align with your budget goals. Dive into the world of generative AI with confidence, knowing that you have the tools to navigate and control the financial aspects of your project.
