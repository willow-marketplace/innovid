## Overview

Imagine if work focused on outcomes, not tedium. Imagine if generative AI could empower skilled professionals to embrace their passions, while also working. Our panel of founders are doing just that! Hear from Crate, Knowtex, Fireworks, and Yellow on how they're leveraging genAI to make work more enjoyable, rewarding, and productive. From automating repetitive tasks, to enhancing collaboration, discover genAI's implementation and its role in achieving exceptional results, and how you can inspire positive change in your own business.
