## PhysicsX: Accelerating Engineering Innovation with Generative AI

[PhysicsX](https://www.physicsx.ai/) is transforming how engineers design, test, and operate complex systems and machines, replacing traditional numerical simulation with AI-powered inference to dramatically accelerate product development.

Running detailed numerical simulations to predict physical behavior typically demands significant time and resources. However, it's critical for engineers building anything from planes and electric cars to semiconductors and consumer electronics solutions.

PhysicsX collaborated with AWS to create an enterprise AI platform that drastically accelerates this process and enables a more efficient path from digital design to real-world performance. It's allowing engineers to run simulations at speed and scale, and to test and optimize designs more cost-effectively.

> "Being a startup, you have to move very fast. AWS has been great, providing us compute power quickly, and helping us interact with customers effectively in the marketplace."
>
> — Robin Tuluie, Ph.D., Founder & Chairman

Built on the AWS cloud, the PhysicsX platform replaces traditional numerical simulation with high-performing physics-based AI foundation models, powered by AWS Elastic Kubernetes Service, with models trained using AWS Batch. The startup was able to easily and cost-effectively access the AWS services through AWS Activate, the [AWS Generative AI Accelerator](https://aws.amazon.com/startups/learn/announcing-the-80-startups-selected-for-the-2024-aws-generative-ai-accelerator), and the [Clean Energy Accelerator](https://aws.amazon.com/energy-utilities/clean-energy-accelerator/), allowing it to develop and scale its platform and model training infrastructure, and reach more customers as a result.

Organizations in sectors spanning automotive, aerospace & defense, materials, semiconductors, and energy, can now address some of the most complex challenges in engineering, using the platform to maximize performance and compress traditional development timelines.
