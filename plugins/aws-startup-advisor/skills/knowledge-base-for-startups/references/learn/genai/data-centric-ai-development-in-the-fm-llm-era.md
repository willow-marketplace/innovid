## Data-centric AI development in the FM/LLM era

## Overview

Snorkel AI co-founder Paroma Varma and founding engineer Vincent Chen will explore the powerful world of generative AI tools and ways to adapt ML models of all sizes—including multi-billion parameter foundation models—to custom use cases over private/proprietary data + tasks. Paroma and Vincent discuss overcoming last mile AI development challenges with innovative techniques like weak supervision and iterative data-centric workflows that help users customize LLMs using their own data up to 10-100x more efficiently. From harnessing the power of AI-driven data augmentation to leveraging advanced generative models, you'll learn cutting-edge approaches to help you deliver efficient, production-ready models.
