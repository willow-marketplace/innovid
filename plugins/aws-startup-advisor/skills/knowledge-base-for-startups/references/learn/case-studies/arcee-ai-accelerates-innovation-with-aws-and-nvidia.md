## Arcee AI Accelerates Innovation with AWS and NVIDIA

_Source: [AWS Startups](/startups/learn/arcee-ai-accelerates-innovation-with-aws-and-nvidia)_

---

Discover how [Arcee AI](https://www.arcee.ai/) is driving AI innovation through its strategic collaboration with AWS and NVIDIA. In this fast-paced conversation, Davis Stone, Head of Partnerships, shares how Arcee evolved its software platform and launched Conductor—an intelligent model router—from concept to production in under 30 days, powered by AWS.

Arcee AI leverages [Amazon SageMaker HyperPod](https://aws.amazon.com/sagemaker/ai/hyperpod/) for efficient training, and powers inference with Amazon EC2 G6e instances accelerated by NVIDIA L40S GPUs. Their AI observability solutions enable enterprises to rapidly experiment with multiple models, improve latency, and unlock high performance at scale.

Arcee AI is available in [AWS Marketplace](https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/seller-profile?id=seller-r7b33ivdczgs6), fully deployed in the AWS Cloud, and accelerated by NVIDIA—empowering teams to build, monitor, and optimize agentic and LLM applications for real-world impact. The company is a member of [NVIDIA's Inception Program](https://www.centralcomputer.com/nvidia-inception-program-gpu-discount) and [AWS Activate](https://aws.amazon.com/activate/activate-landing/), gaining access to cutting-edge AI/ML resources, preferred GPU pricing, cloud credits, and ongoing technical and business support tailored for startup growth.

Learn more at the [AWS and NVIDIA](https://aws.amazon.com/nvidia/) hub.
