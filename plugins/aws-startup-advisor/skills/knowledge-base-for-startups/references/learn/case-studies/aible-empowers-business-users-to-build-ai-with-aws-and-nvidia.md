## Aible Empowers Business Users to Build AI with AWS and NVIDIA

_Source: [AWS Startups](/startups/learn)_

---

[Aible AI](https://www.aible.com/) is empowering business users to build and train their own AI agents using their own data, directly in their own cloud environments. Founder Arijit Sengupta shares how Aible's platform democratizes AI by letting anyone start from templates, give real-time feedback to agents, and iterate solutions rapidly—no data science PhD required.

## AWS and NVIDIA Integration

Learn how Aible leverages AWS and NVIDIA to accelerate innovation:

- **NVIDIA Grace Hopper and Grace Blackwell architectures** enable massively efficient reasoning and retrieval-augmented generation by running everything on a single Superchip
- **AWS** offers the scale, reliability, and native integrations that Aible's customers demand

Projects that once took months and millions of dollars can now be achieved in hours at a fraction of the cost.

## Real Results

Hear real results from Aible implementations:

- Helped the **state of Nebraska** build a productive agent in just **2.5 hours**
- Delivered custom AI for **health systems** in under **48 hours**
- Fine-tuned advanced LLMs for enterprise tasks:
  - 8B parameters at 82% accuracy in 22 minutes for $4.58
  - 70B parameters at 92% accuracy in just over an hour for $11.30

## Architecture and Security

Aible's flexible architecture keeps data private, running on DGX Cloud on AWS for seamless, secure AI workflows. The company is a member of:

- [NVIDIA's Inception Program](https://www.centralcomputer.com/nvidia-inception-program-gpu-discount)
- [AWS Activate Startup Program](https://aws.amazon.com/activate/activate-landing/)

Aible is pioneering next-gen AI at the edge for telco and retail, combining privacy, performance, and minimal data movement.
