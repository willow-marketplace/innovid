## Founder Fundamentals

Building a startup is no easy feat. Understand the steps to take turning your big idea into a business.

## Course Details

This course covers the essential topics for startup founders. Each module is designed to help you progress through key stages of building your startup.

### Course Modules

1. **Getting started as a founder** (2 mins)
2. **Finding a co-founder** (3 mins)
3. **Turning your startup idea into a reality** (2 mins)
4. **How to find and listen to customers** (3 mins)
5. **Customer validation and understanding your market** (4 mins)
6. **Building a prototype** (3 mins)
