## Weights and Biases is helping developers productionize AI solutions and realize AI innovation

> **Source:** [AWS Startups](https://startups.aws/startups/learn/weights-and-biases-is-helping-developers-productionize-ai-solutions-and-realize-ai-innovation)

## Overview

From a single line of code to an application, agent, or model: [Weights & Biases](https://wandb.ai/site/) provides the tools developers need to productionize AI ideas and realize business value from AI innovation. Both Weights & Biases and its customers rely on advanced tools and services from AWS to power secure solutions that foster collaboration and protect IP.

AI engineers and machine learning (ML) teams are the driving force behind next-generation technology. However, Weights & Biases identified a significant gap: developer tools specifically designed for the AI workflow. The company's solution offers a single pane-of-glass approach, allowing entire teams to work together and better track, understand, improve, and monitor their AI-powered applications.

Weights & Biases is also addressing a second challenge: security. "Many of our customers are very security conscious, and when you're working on AI, you're often using very sensitive data that's really important to the business's core IP," says Chris Van Pelt, Co-Founder and Chief Information Security Officer.

The company has been working with AWS "since the very beginning", using advanced security controls and custom hardware accelerators and related services, such as [AWS Trainium](https://aws.amazon.com/ai/machine-learning/trainium/) and [AWS Inferentia](https://aws.amazon.com/ai/machine-learning/inferentia/).

Developers need to work quickly to productionize and deploy AI, and partnering with AWS has enabled Weights & Biases to keep pace with its customers, explains Van Pelt: "it's really impressive as an engineer to see how quickly AWS is able to create cutting-edge services that really change the landscape."

As such, the company has been able to "surf this wave of expansive growth of ML ops and AI systems." This is bringing benefits to the leading enterprises and developer teams the company works with, and having "transformational" outcomes for Weights & Biases' business.
